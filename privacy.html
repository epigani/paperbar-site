Privacy Policy

PaperBar has no account system and no PaperBar server.

PaperBar is designed as a local macOS utility. It does not include telemetry, advertising trackers, analytics SDKs, or subscription infrastructure.

Local tools

Table conversion, BibTeX cleaning, QR generation, clipboard actions, and PDF text extraction run on your Mac.

Network access

DOI and citation lookup can contact Crossref to fetch public bibliographic metadata. PaperBar does not proxy that request through a PaperBar backend.

Files and clipboard

PaperBar reads files and clipboard contents only when you choose to paste, import, drag, or export.

Contact

Questions can be sent to contact@paperbar.app.
